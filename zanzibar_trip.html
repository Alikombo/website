Kite lessons



Steps to learn

What will you learn with us and what your lessons include. Average person goes through all of these stages in 10 hours (that is why we recommend 10 hour package, so you can learn to kite really well):
You will get full equipment for kiting and a qualified instructor that will teach you these steps:

Hour no. 1
  • Balancing of trainer kite and learning about the wind. How to select a proper kite for different winds and how to know when wind is good for kiting.
  • How to set up a kite - how to connect the bar with kite, pump the kite and install your harness in a proper way to make sure, your kiting experience is always fun but also safe.
  • Learning safety systems. Instructor teaches you what to do in cases when wind pulls you so you remain safe.
    • Hour no. 2 and 3
  • Balancing the big kite in water. Here you will learn how to control your kite so that it pulls you to the right or left side. You will also learn how to walk with kite left, right and back. Besides this you will learn how you can use the kite to drag you to the shore in the direction of downwind and how you can control kite with one hand to drag you upwind left and right.
    • Hour no. 4
  • Water relaunching - you will learn how to pick up kite from water when it falls down without the help of instructor. This is very important for your later individual kite experiences.
    • Hour no. 5 and 6 (or more if needed)
  • Learning to water-start with the board. You will learn how to use the power of wind in your kite to pull you out of the water enough so you can start boarding! This may take some time, but never worry.
  • Learning how to go left and right down-wind with sitting down before turning
    • Further hours
  • learning how to go in the opposite direction of the wind (upwind) - you will learn how to position your body and legs and how to really use the full power of the kite.
  • standing transition - you will learn how to turn your kite without sitting in the water
  • if you know all of this you can also learn to do a small jump or a special move called 12side (when you do the turn, you also switch the legs - another leg gets in the front).

Most of my students who buy a package of 10 hours learn at least 8 of these steps in this amount of time. Maybe you will need more or less time, but that depends on your sport background, talent and on your confidence.
Remember - we were all once beginners so just relax and enjoy the process.
