Zanzibar activities

Sports activities (skateboarding, acrobatics etc.)

If you are into sports, you can book a private hour with me, where I teach you how to do bboy or some acrobatic elements or you can go with me to learn how to skateboard. If you are interested in these activities, write me a message and we will arrange a good plan for your goals and needs.

Safari Blue

Full day trip. 9 am in the morning, car picks you up in Paje and takes you to a place where you hop on the board of a big wooden ship with wich you visit a sand bank for a fruit snack and later you do amazing snorkeling to see tropical fish. In the middle of the day you are served a big lunch with a wide range of sea food, potato, rice, salad and vegetable souce plus you go to see a huge bau bau tree located on a remote island.

Jozani Forest

3 hour trip. Car picks you up in Paje from where you have a 20 min ride to Jozani forest. You visit a protected forest where you can see a special kind of monkeys (red back monkeys) and the guide also teaches you different things about trees that grow on the island and their healing properties. After you are done, the car takes you back.

Nakupenda and Prison island

A full day trip. Car picks you up in Paje and takes you to stone town. There, you board on a small boat that first takes you to prison island where you can see a historic prison and gigantic tortoise and you can also feed them. After that you get back on the boat and head towards a sandbank called Nakupenda (I love you), where you can enjoy your day and swim. This trip also includes a lunch of seafood and potatoes and fresh fruits. Our boat captains will also take care of the shadow above your head, so you don't get burned under the African sun. You will return to stone town at around 4pm and the driver will take you back to your accommodation.

Swimming with dolphins

Morning trip. Early in the morning (around 5) car will pick you up in Paje and take you to Kizimkaze where you will go far away to the ocean and will be able to go to swim with the dolphins. After the experience, the driver will take you back to your accommodation

Spice tour

4 hour trip. You will visit an amazing area near Stone Town where Zanzibarians are planting fruits, herbs, vegetables and other plant remedies for the whole island. An experienced guide will inform you about different plants and what they can be used for and how. At the end of the trip you will get a chance to try many different tropical fruits so make sure you come to this trip hungry.
