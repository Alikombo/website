MY STORY

Hey, my name is Hassan and I've always been a big fan of sports. I like to do acrobatics, breakdance and for the past 7 years, my big passion is also kite.

My philosophy as an instructor is, that I want to teach my students to learn how to kite with the least hours as possible.

Nothing makes me more happy, then seeing my students kitesurfing on their own quickly.

However, the one thing that is also very important for me is safety and good conditions. I always try to enable my students good conditions to learn how to kite and I am very careful to keep them safe. Kite surfing is a wonderful sport, but you have to learn how to be careful not to hit swimmers or other kite-surfers/boats etc.

Most of my students become good friends of mine, sometimes we also go to party together or we do one of the many amazing Zanzibar trips.
